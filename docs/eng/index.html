About me

My name is Maxim. I'm Front-End & Wordpress developer and little web designer from Bryansk, Russia. I build bespoke websites to help my clients push their businesses further online.

Since beginning my journey as a web-developer passed over 2 years and during this time I managed to work on several interesting projects.

I own and use the following technologies and tools: HTML5, CSS3, SASS (SCSS), JavaScript, jQuery, Bootstrap, Gulp, Webpack, Git, BEM, Photoshop, Illustrator, PHP and PhpStorm or Sublime Text as the main IDE.
